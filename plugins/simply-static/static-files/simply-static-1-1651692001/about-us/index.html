Here is some information about us

Our private health mental clinic was founded in 1996 by Dr Laura Briges. It is since then we have been fighting with the mental health issues of our patients. We are a teamfull of motivation, enthusiasm and commitment and we believe that there are no insurmountable problems.

Strong minds Foundation

In order to provide even more help, we established a Strong minds foundation which supports people suffering from depression. We’re so happy that we can help in many other ways!
